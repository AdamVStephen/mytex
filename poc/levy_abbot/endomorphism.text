\centerline{\bf 3. Endomorphism of an $A$-module of finite type}
\medskip
\noindent Theorem 3.1.
{\it If $M$ is an $A$-module of finite type and $u:M\rightarrow M$ is an endomorphism of $M$,
the following equivalence holds:
$$
u \hbox{ is surjective}\iff u \hbox{ is bijective.}
$$
